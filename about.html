About Me

Hi, I'm Isabella!

Bringing imagination to life through the vibrant worlds of games and animation is my passion. As a design student majoring in these exciting fields of games and animation, I'm a 3D artist and illustrator dedicated to crafting compelling visuals. Through my studies and personal projects, I've gained experience in arts and 3D modeling, specializing in creating both stylized and realistic 3D models for games, animation, and interactive media.

When I'm not immersed in the digital realm of Blender, you can find me in my studio, exploring new artistic ideas through painting or further refining my 3D skills with a fun project in Blender. I invite you to explore my portfolio below to see my work in action
