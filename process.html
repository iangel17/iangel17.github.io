My Process

Every project begins with a idea, a concept and a simple reference image. I then move through modelling, lighting, texturing and final presentation.

1. Research

I collect reference images and decide the style, mood and proportions before starting the 3D model.

2. Modelling

I block out the basic shapes first, refining the details bit by bit, refering to the images using mainly Blender.

3. Materials

Utilising textures, paint colours combined with UV mapping are adjusted to make the model feel complete.

4. Render

Lighting, camera angle and composition are added, tested and adjusted before exporting the final image.
